You will see a context word (e.g. CAT). 
Then, you will see a really fast series of letter strings (e.g. FGHBVDT), one after the other.
One of them contains a target word (e.g. XXDOGXX) in red. 
Decide whether this target word is related or unrelated to the context word.
(e.g. CAT- DOG, and BRIGHT-DARK are related pairs, but CAT-SKY are not related)

Unrelated/Related
– If you see an UNRELATED word, press the F key when you see  the second “?”
– If you see a RELATED word, press the J key when you see  the second “?”

Please focus your attention and respond as accurately and swiftly as possible. 
Press SPACE to start.
